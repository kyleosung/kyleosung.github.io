Research

Currently I'm doing research in Data Science and Machine Learning on charging cycles of lithium batteries. The GitHub repo is private at the moment, but we are making use of the following Python modules: scikit-learn, scipy, pysindy, pandas, numpy, matplotlib, and more.

In the future I hope to work on more datasci projects in Deep Learning and Statistics. I also hope to explore areas of Pure Math such as Number Theory and Analysis.

If you would like to collaborate on a research project, please feel free to reach out!
